About Valid

Valid is the anonymous polling app that brings your high school together through fun, positive, and honest questions. Answer daily polls about your friends and classmates, earn aura points, and see who's voting for you while staying anonymous.

How It Works

  • Answer daily questions by selecting from your contacts and classmates.
  • Questions are anonymous, unless you choose to reveal.
  • Earn aura points for every answer.
  • See votes you receive in your personal feed.
  • Browse school-wide activity to see what's trending.
