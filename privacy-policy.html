Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Valid collects, uses, and protects information when you use our app, website, and related services.

Information We Collect

  • Account information, such as your phone number, name, age, school, grade, username, and profile details.
  • Contact information you choose to share so we can help you find classmates, invite friends, and power social features.
  • Poll, vote, invite, reveal, subscription, purchase, and app activity information.
  • Photos or profile images you choose to upload.
  • Device, log, analytics, diagnostic, and notification token information used to operate and improve the service.

How We Use Information

  • To create and secure your account.
  • To run polls, invites, reveals, notifications, subscriptions, and other app features.
  • To personalize your experience based on your school, contacts, and app activity.
  • To prevent spam, abuse, fraud, and unsafe behavior.
  • To troubleshoot, analyze performance, and improve Valid.

Contacts

If you grant contact access, we use contact data to help identify people you may know, show relevant classmates, and support invite flows. You can change contact permissions in your device settings.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers that help us host the service, verify phone numbers, process purchases, send notifications, store data, measure app performance, and keep the service secure. We may also share information when required by law or to protect our users, rights, and service.

Subscriptions And Purchases

Purchases and subscriptions are processed by Apple. We receive transaction and entitlement information needed to unlock paid features, validate purchases, and provide customer support.

Data Retention

We keep information for as long as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, prevent abuse, and enforce our agreements. You may request account deletion from within the app where available.

Children

Valid is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information to us, contact us so we can review and take appropriate action.

Security

We use technical and organizational safeguards designed to protect information, but no online service can guarantee absolute security.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent to support@validapp.lol.
